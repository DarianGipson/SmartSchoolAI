# AI Content Moderation and Logging Policy

SmartSchool AI is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and educational environment. To achieve this, we:

- Log all user interactions with the AI for auditability, transparency, and safety.
- Use automated and manual review to detect and filter harmful, biased, or inappropriate content.
- Regularly update our moderation algorithms to improve accuracy and reduce bias.
- Respond promptly to user reports of inappropriate content.

If you encounter problematic content, please contact support@smartschool.ai.
